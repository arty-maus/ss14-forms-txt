[bold][head=2]Section IV: Standard Grounds for Denial[/head][/bold]

[italic][color=#aaaaaa]In the interest of transparency, your request may be regretfully declined for any of the following reasons, entirely at the discretion of the Office of the Head of Personnel:[/color][/italic]

 - Requested access exceeds, equals, or falls below that which is deemed appropriate.
 - Petitioner's name does not match the name on file, the name on the badge, or the name the reviewing officer had in mind.
 - Form submitted on the incorrect shade of paper.
 - Reason for request was too detailed, insufficiently detailed, or detailed in the wrong order.
 - Petitioner has previously been granted access to something, at some point, by someone.
 - The requested area does not require access, want access, or is not aware it has been requested.
 - Stamp ink assessed as spiritually, if not chemically, incorrect.
 - Request filed on a day ending in "y."
 - Reviewing officer's tea was unsatisfactory at time of review.
 - No reason provided. No reason required.
